# GLOBAL GAMING & ESPORTS GROWTH 2010-2025 ANALYSIS DASHBOARD
Power BI dashboard analyzing Global Gaming &amp; Esports Growth (2010–2025), focusing on revenue trends, esports tournaments, viewer growth, platform performance, and genre dominance.

## Project Overview
- This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing the growth of the global gaming and esports industry from 2010 to 2025.
- The dashboard focuses on gaming revenue, esports tournaments, viewer growth, platform performance, and game genres.
- The purpose of this project is to understand industry trends and visualize how the gaming ecosystem has evolved over time.

## Objectives
- Analyze global gaming revenue trends from 2010 to 2025
- Study the growth of esports tournaments and viewers
- Compare the performance of gaming platforms such as PC, Console, and Mobile
- Identify the most popular gaming genres
- Generate insights that help gaming companies, investors, and developers understand market trends

## Key Insights
- Gaming revenue shows steady growth from 2010 to 2025
- The industry experienced faster growth after 2018 due to streaming platforms and mobile gaming
- Mobile gaming platforms show strong tournament growth
- Asia generates the highest gaming revenue while Europe hosts more tournaments
- FPS and RPG genres are the most dominant and attract the highest number of viewers

## Tools and Technologies Used
- Microsoft Excel for data cleaning and preparation
- Power BI for data visualization and dashboard development

## Business Impact
- Helps gaming companies identify high growth regions
- Helps investors understand gaming market trends
- Helps developers focus on popular game genres
