# esports & gaming-data-visualization-dashboard
Power BI dashboard analyzing Global Gaming &amp; Esports Growth (2010–2025), focusing on revenue trends, esports tournaments, viewer growth, platform performance, and genre dominance.

This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard that analyzes the growth of the global gaming and esports industry from 2010 to 2025.

The dashboard focuses on key metrics such as gaming revenue, esports tournaments, esports revenue, viewer growth, platform performance, and game genres.

The goal of this project is to understand how the gaming ecosystem has evolved and identify industry trends, regional performance, and dominant gaming genres using data visualization.
----
🎯 Objectives
 - Analyze global gaming revenue trends from 2010 to 2025
- Study the growth of esports tournaments and viewers
- Compare the performance of gaming platforms such as PC, Console, and Mobile
- Identify the most popular gaming genres
- Generate insights that can help gaming companies, investors, and developers understand market trends
----
📈 Key Insights
Gaming revenue shows consistent growth from 2010 to 2025.
The industry growth accelerated significantly after 2018.
Mobile gaming is rapidly expanding with strong tournament activity.
Asia generates the highest gaming revenue, while Europe hosts many tournaments.
FPS and RPG genres dominate in terms of popularity and viewer engagement.
